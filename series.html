The Show

The Series

The Mandalorian is an American space Western television series created by Jon Favreau for Disney+. Set in the Star Wars universe, it follows the adventures of a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy.

Created By

Jon Favreau

Jon Favreau created The Mandalorian, drawing inspiration from classic Westerns and samurai films. The show revolutionized television production with its use of the StageCraft virtual production technology, also known as "The Volume."
