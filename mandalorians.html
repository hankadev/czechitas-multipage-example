Who Are The Mandalorians?

The Mandalorians are a proud warrior people whose culture dates back thousands of years. Originating from the planet Mandalore, they have spread across the galaxy, united not by blood but by creed.

Beskar Armor

Mandalorian armor is forged from beskar, one of the strongest metals in the galaxy. It can withstand blaster fire and even lightsaber strikes.

Warrior Culture

Mandalorians are raised as warriors from birth. Combat training is central to their identity, making them some of the most feared fighters in the galaxy.

The Creed

Mandalorians follow a strict code known as "The Way." Traditional Mandalorians never remove their helmets in front of others.

Foundlings

Mandalorians adopt orphans of war, known as foundlings. These children are raised in the Way and become full Mandalorians.
